WHAT IS "GRÁFICA POPULAR"?

"Gráfica Popular" or Popular Graphic is a form of communication and design. Popular Graphic represents different cultures and can also be compared in different areas.

Every country and sometimes even in different areas of different countries, have different perceptions of different things, and different perceptions of objects and how they work/how they are used. All of this depending on their environment.

The way things are created and the processing of it depends on the resources available this is what shapes the Popular Graphic of an area. It is also shaped by the way people perceive things; it comes through in their graphics and their process of creating the graphics.

Ex: the materials and the tools chosen, and how they are used.
