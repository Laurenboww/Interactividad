On-The-Go

Any place, any time the FotoSet App is easy to use and a fast solution for those on-the-go. Use it at events to get your subject's names and emails.

Keep it Clean

In your "Event Library" you can see all of your events ordered by date; latest event albums first. This helps us photographers keep our content organized.

Tag 'Em!

The name tags option allows you to keep your subject's name's in order. This could be looked at later for publishings & posting purposes.

Share Your Events

Once you're finished with the name tags and notes you can share entire event albums with clients, business associates, magazine partners, etc.
